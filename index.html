About

I am a researcher at Princeton University working with Andrea Goldsmith and H. Vincent Poor. I received my Ph.D. in Electrical and Computer Engineering from the University of Arizona in 2022.

My research lies at the intersection of information theory, machine learning, and wireless systems, with a focus on privacy-preserving and communication-efficient AI.

More recently, I have been investigating statistical learning problems in large language models (LLMs), focusing on designing fine-grained watermarking algorithms that enable post-generation edit detection and ensure content integrity.
